### Hi there! :) ☀️

I'm Felix, an **ML engineer with research experience**, who has worked across adversarial ML and LLM security, soft robotics, and machine learning for protein and materials engineering.

Having built and deployed ML systems at the frontier of biology and materials science, I am drawn to understanding and mitigating the unintended consequences of increasingly capable AI, and am now **transitioning towards AI alignment and safety research**.

I am currently finishing my **M.Sc. in Computer Science at TU Berlin**. Previously, I worked as a** ML Engineer at Cambrium**, building generative-protein and materials-engineering tools, and have also carried out research on adversarial ML, LLM-based security, and model transfer in soft robotics. I hold a **B.Sc. in Informatics from the Technical University of Munich (TUM)** and an **Honors Degree in Technology Management from the Center for Digital Technology and Management (CDTM)**.
